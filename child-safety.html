Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Flap is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that harms, exploits, or endangers minors.

Flap is a private app where users create shared spaces with friends to share images, notes, files, polls, and tasks. All content shared on Flap must follow strict safety standards.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual or suggestive content involving minors
  • Content that exploits, harms, or endangers children
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to contact minors for exploitation
  • Sharing, requesting, or promoting inappropriate content involving minors

Enforcement Actions

We actively review and respond to violations of these policies.

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Temporary or permanent suspension of user accounts
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities when required

Reporting Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that may violate child safety standards, please report it immediately.

  • Use in-app reporting features
  • Contact us directly via email
  • Provide relevant details such as screenshots or user information if possible

All reports are reviewed with high priority.

Platform Safety Measures

Flap is designed with safety and privacy in mind:

  • No public feed or global content discovery
  • Content is shared only within private spaces
  • Only invited members can access a space
  • Users control who they interact with
  • Ability to report harmful or unsafe content
  • Monitoring and moderation systems to detect misuse

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they create and share. You must not upload or share any content involving minors that violates safety standards.
