Child Safety & CSAE Standards

CampusThreads is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We do not allow any content that involves, promotes, or exploits minors in any sexual or harmful way.

Our Standards

  • We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any sexual content involving minors.
  • We do not allow content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers children.
  • We take appropriate action against accounts that violate these rules, including content removal and account termination.
  • We cooperate with legal authorities when required by law.

Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report inappropriate or abusive content within the app. We review reports and take action according to our policies. Content that violates child safety standards is removed as soon as possible.
