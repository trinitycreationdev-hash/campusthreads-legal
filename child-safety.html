Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Flap is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that harms or exploits minors.

Flap is a private collection-based platform where users share content within selected groups. All content must follow strict safety standards.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Content that exploits, harms, or endangers children
  • Grooming behavior or attempts to contact minors for exploitation
  • Sharing or requesting inappropriate content involving minors

Enforcement Actions

We actively monitor and respond to violations of these policies.

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent suspension of offending accounts
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities when required

Reporting Safety Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that violates child safety standards, please report it immediately.

  • Use in-app reporting tools
  • Contact us directly via email

All reports are reviewed and handled with priority.

Platform Safety Measures

Flap is designed with safety in mind:

  • Private collections with controlled access
  • No public content discovery
  • Limited sharing within trusted groups
  • Tools to report misuse or harmful behavior
