Child Safety & CSAE Standards

Cleever is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We strictly prohibit any content that involves, promotes, or exploits minors in a sexual or harmful way.

Our Standards

  • We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors.
  • We do not allow content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers children.
  • We prohibit grooming behavior, predatory conduct, or any attempt to exploit minors.
  • Accounts that violate these rules may be permanently suspended or removed.
  • We cooperate with legal authorities when required by law.

Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report harmful or abusive content directly within the Cleever app. This includes reporting posts, messages, comments, or users that violate our community standards.

Our team reviews reports and takes appropriate action, including content removal, account suspension, or reporting to relevant authorities when required.

Community Moderation

Cleever provides moderation tools to help maintain a safe community environment. Users can report inappropriate content, and space owners can manage their communities by removing members who violate the rules.
