Privacy Policy for CampusThreads

Last updated: 03 March 2026

CampusThreads ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the CampusThreads mobile application and related services (the "Service").

By using CampusThreads, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Eligibility

CampusThreads is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
  • If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the Service.

a) Information You Provide

  • Phone number (used for authentication and login)
  • Username
  • College or university name (optional)
  • Profile avatar or emoji (optional)
  • Posts, comments, likes, and content you publish
  • Feedback or reports submitted by you

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Basic usage information
  • Crash logs and error reports

This information helps us maintain, secure, and improve the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your login using your phone number
  • Allow you to create posts and interact with other users
  • Display content according to your visibility settings
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent spam, abuse, or misuse
  • Respond to feedback or support requests

We do not sell your personal data.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

CampusThreads uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication – for phone number login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore – for storing user profiles, posts, comments, and interactions
  • Firebase Storage – for storing uploaded images

Your information is stored securely on Google's cloud infrastructure using industry-standard security practices.

5. Visibility of Your Content

  • Your username, avatar, posts, and comments may be visible to other users.
  • When posting content you may choose:
    • Public – visible to all users
    • My College – visible only to users who selected the same college
  • Your phone number is never visible to other users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service.

Backup copies of data may exist temporarily for security, legal, or operational reasons.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account.

When your account is deleted:

  • Your authentication account is removed
  • Your profile data is deleted from our database
  • Your posts may be deleted or anonymized
  • Backup data may remain temporarily for security purposes

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

9. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Secure Firebase security rules
  • Encrypted network connections (HTTPS)
  • Restricted administrative access

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

10. Third-Party Services

CampusThreads relies on third-party services such as Google Firebase to provide authentication, database, and cloud storage services.

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell user data
  • We do not show advertisements (currently)
  • We do not track users across other apps
  • We do not collect financial or government ID information
  • We do not collect precise location data

12. Children's Privacy

  • CampusThreads is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If discovered, such data will be removed

13. Web Version (Cookies / Local Storage)

The web version may use cookies or local storage to maintain login sessions and essential functionality.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the Service indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
