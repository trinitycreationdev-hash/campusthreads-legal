Privacy Policy for Flap

Last updated: 19 April 2026

Flap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Flap app.

Flap is designed as a private sharing platform where users create collections and share content with selected people.

1. Eligibility

  • Flap is intended for users aged 13 and above.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Username and profile information
  • Profile image (optional)
  • Collections you create
  • Photos and content you upload
  • Short text entries (logs or descriptions)
  • People you invite to collections
  • Feedback or reports you submit

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and OS
  • App version
  • Basic usage data
  • Crash logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable collection creation and sharing
  • Allow you to upload and store images
  • Enable private sharing with selected members
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Prevent misuse and ensure security

We do not sell your personal data.

4. How Data Is Stored

We use secure cloud services including:

  • Firebase Authentication (login)
  • Cloud Firestore (data storage)
  • Firebase Storage (images)

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.

5. Content Visibility

  • Collections are private by default.
  • Only people invited to a collection can view its content.
  • There is no public feed or global visibility.
  • Your phone number is never visible to other users.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the service. Backup copies may be stored temporarily.

7. Account Deletion

  • You can request deletion of your account.
  • Your collections and data will be removed.
  • Some data may remain temporarily in backups.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Request deletion
  • Contact us for concerns

9. Security

  • Secure authentication
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to systems

No system is completely secure, but we take strong precautions.

10. Third-Party Services

We use services like Google Firebase to operate the app. These services may process data according to their own policies.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not show ads (currently)
  • We do not track you across other apps
  • We do not collect financial or sensitive identity data
  • We do not collect precise GPS location

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

13. Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of India.
