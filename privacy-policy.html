Privacy Policy for Flap

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Flap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Flap mobile application.

Flap is a private app that allows users to create shared spaces with their friends to share images, notes, files, polls, and tasks.

1. Eligibility

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use Flap.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Your name or username
  • Profile image (optional)
  • Content you create within spaces (images, notes, files, polls, to-do items)
  • Spaces you create or join
  • Interactions (votes, completions, views, etc.)

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (for performance improvements)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Push notification token (for updates and activity alerts)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable you to create and use private shared spaces
  • Allow you to share and view content within your spaces
  • Show your content to members of your spaces
  • Enable collaboration (notes, polls, tasks, files)
  • Send notifications related to activity in your spaces
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal data.

4. How Data Is Stored

We use secure cloud services including:

  • Firebase Firestore (user data and content)
  • Firebase Storage (images and files)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications)

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.

5. How Sharing Works

  • Your content is visible only to members of the space.
  • Spaces are private and accessible only by invited users.
  • There is no public feed or global visibility.

6. Data Retention

  • Your content is stored as long as your account or space exists.
  • Deleted content may remain temporarily in backups.

7. Account and Data Deletion

  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • This includes your profile, spaces, and content.
  • Some data may remain temporarily in backups for system reliability.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Request deletion
  • Contact us for any concerns

9. Security

  • Secure data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to systems
  • Regular monitoring and updates

While we take strong precautions, no system is completely secure.

10. Third-Party Services

Flap uses services such as Google Firebase to operate the app. These services may process data according to their own policies.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not show ads (currently)
  • We do not track you across other apps
  • We do not collect financial information
  • We do not collect precise location data

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

13. Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of India.
