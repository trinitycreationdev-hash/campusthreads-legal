Privacy Policy for Flap

Last updated: 17 April 2026

Flap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Flap mobile application and related services (the "Service").

By using Flap, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Eligibility

Flap is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
  • If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve the Service.

a) Information You Provide

  • Phone number (used for authentication and login)
  • Username
  • Profile avatar or emoji (optional)
  • Posts, comments, and content you share
  • Images and media you upload
  • Optional location you choose to add to posts
  • Likes, interactions, and replies
  • Feedback or reports submitted by you

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Basic usage information
  • Crash logs and error reports

This information helps us maintain, secure, and improve the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your login using your phone number
  • Allow you to create posts and interact with other users
  • Enable conversations through replies and interactions
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent spam, abuse, or misuse
  • Respond to feedback or support requests

We do not sell your personal data.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Flap uses Google Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication – for phone number login
  • Cloud Firestore – for storing user data, posts, comments, and interactions
  • Firebase Storage – for storing uploaded images

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure.

5. Visibility of Your Content

  • Your username, profile, posts, and comments may be visible to other users.
  • You can control who sees your posts using visibility options:
    • Everyone – visible to all users
    • Following – visible only to users who follow you
  • Any location you add to a post is visible along with that post.
  • Your phone number is never visible to other users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service.

Backup copies may be retained temporarily for operational and security purposes.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account.

  • Your account and profile data will be deleted
  • Your posts may be deleted or anonymized
  • Some data may remain temporarily in backups

8. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

9. Data Security

  • Secure Firebase rules
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Restricted system access

No system is completely secure, but we take strong precautions.

10. Third-Party Services

Flap uses services like Google Firebase for authentication, storage, and database functionality. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell user data
  • We do not show ads (currently)
  • We do not track users across other apps
  • We do not collect financial or government ID information
  • We do not collect precise GPS location

12. Children's Privacy

  • Not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect their data
  • If found, it will be deleted

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted here.
