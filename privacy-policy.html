Privacy Policy for CampusThreads

Last updated:

CampusThreads ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the CampusThreads mobile app and web app (the "Service").

By using CampusThreads, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who Can Use CampusThreads

CampusThreads is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve the Service.

a) Information You Provide

  • Name or username
  • Email address
  • College or university name (optional)
  • Profile photo or avatar emoji (optional)
  • Posts, comments, likes, and images you upload
  • Feedback or reports you submit

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type and basic device information
  • App version
  • Approximate usage data
  • Crash and error logs (to improve stability and performance)

This information is mainly collected through Google Firebase services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow you to create posts, comments, and upload images
  • Display your content based on your chosen visibility (public or college)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Protect the Service from abuse, spam, or misuse
  • Respond to feedback and support requests

We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

CampusThreads uses Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication – for login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore – to store user profiles, posts, comments, and likes
  • Firebase Storage – to store images such as profile photos and post images

Your data is stored on Google’s servers and protected using industry-standard security measures.

5. Who Can See Your Content

  • Your username, avatar (emoji or photo), posts, and comments may be visible to other users.
  • When creating a post, you can choose:
    • Public – visible to all users
    • My College – visible only to users with the same college (if you added one)
  • Your email address is not shown publicly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Backup copies of data may be stored for a limited period for security, legal, or technical reasons and are automatically deleted after that period.

7. Deleting Your Account and Data

You can request to delete your account from within the app.

When you delete your account:

  • Your authentication account is removed
  • Your profile data is removed from our database
  • Your content may be deleted or anonymized
  • Some data may remain in backups for a limited time for legal or security reasons

If you need help, you can contact us to request data deletion.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Request support regarding your data

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:

  • Secure Firebase rules and access controls
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to sensitive operations

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Third-Party Services

CampusThreads uses third-party services, mainly Google Firebase. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not show ads (currently)
  • We do not track you across other apps
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial information, or precise location

12. Children’s Privacy

  • CampusThreads is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If such data is found, it will be deleted as soon as possible

13. Web Version (Cookies / Local Storage)

The web version of CampusThreads may use cookies or local storage for login sessions and basic functionality. These are used only to make the Service work properly.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.

15. Legal Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, without regard to its conflict of law principles.
