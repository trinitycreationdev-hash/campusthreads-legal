Privacy Policy for Flap

Last updated: 19 April 2026

Flap ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Flap mobile application.

Flap is a creative app that allows users to take photos, invite friends, combine images into a shared moment, and share results.

1. Eligibility

  • You must be at least 13 years old to use Flap.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Your name (entered in the app)
  • Photos you capture and upload
  • Invite data (links shared with friends)
  • Combined images created with other users

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Push notification token (for reminders and updates)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable you to capture and share photos
  • Allow friends to join and complete poses
  • Generate combined images
  • Send notifications (e.g., pose completion, reminders)
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

We do not sell your personal data.

4. How Data Is Stored

We use secure cloud services including:

  • Firebase Firestore (app data)
  • Firebase Storage (images)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications)

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

5. How Sharing Works

  • When you create a pose, a shareable link is generated.
  • People with the link can access and complete the pose.
  • Combined images may be shared externally (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • We do not provide a public social feed.

6. Data Retention

We store your data as long as needed to provide the service. Some data may remain temporarily in backups for security and reliability.

7. Account and Data Deletion

  • You may request deletion of your data.
  • Uploaded images and related data will be removed.
  • Some data may remain temporarily in backups.

8. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your information
  • Request deletion
  • Contact us for any concerns

9. Security

  • Secure data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to systems
  • Regular monitoring and updates

While we take strong precautions, no system is completely secure.

10. Third-Party Services

Flap uses third-party services such as Google Firebase to operate. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

11. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not show ads (currently)
  • We do not track you across other apps
  • We do not collect financial information
  • We do not collect precise location data

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

13. Jurisdiction

This policy is governed by the laws of India.
