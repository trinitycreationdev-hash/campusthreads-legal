Request Account Deletion

This page is for users of Flap who want to request deletion of their account and associated data.

Important: Once your deletion request is processed, your account and data will be permanently deleted. This process may take up to 30 days.

If you can access the app, we recommend deleting your account directly from the app settings.

How to Delete Your Account from the App

  1. Open the Flap app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm your request

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your account information (username, profile details)
  • Your collections and all associated content
  • Images and media you uploaded
  • Logs or text entries within collections
  • Access to shared collections
  • Your authentication account

Data Retention

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Some data may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations.
