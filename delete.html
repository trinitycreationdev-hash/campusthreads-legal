Request Account Deletion

This page is for users of Flap who want to request deletion of their account and associated data.

Important: Once your deletion request is processed, your account and data will be permanently deleted. Deletions are completed within 30 days for security and verification purposes.

If you can access the app, you can delete your account directly from the app settings. Otherwise, use the option below.

How to Delete Your Account from the App

  1. Open the Flap app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm your choice

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your profile information (username, avatar)
  • Your posts, comments, and uploaded images
  • Your likes and interactions
  • Your authentication account

Data Retention

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days after your request. Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes.

Request Deletion via Email
