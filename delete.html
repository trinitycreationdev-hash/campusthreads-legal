Delete Your Account & Data

Flap allows you to delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app.

Important: Once your account is deleted, your data will be permanently removed. This process may take up to 30 days.

We recommend using the in-app option for faster deletion.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open the Flap app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Open Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (name, profile data)
  • Your profile image
  • Your posts and shared moments
  • Your friend connections
  • Images stored in the app
  • Notification tokens (FCM)

Alternative: Request via Email

If you cannot access your account, you can request deletion by contacting us:

Email: trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your registered account name (if known)
  • Any relevant details to help identify your account

Data Retention

We delete your data within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, or legal requirements.

Important Notes

  • Content shared with others may remain on their devices
  • Deleting your account cannot remove content already saved externally
  • Deleting the app alone does not delete your account data
