How to Delete Your CampusThreads Account

CampusThreads allows all users to permanently delete their account and associated data directly from within the app. No external form or email request is required.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the CampusThreads app
  2. Go to the Settings screen
  3. Tap on Delete account
  4. Confirm your choice

Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, college, avatar/photo)
  • Your posts, comments, and uploaded images
  • Your likes and interactions
  • Your authentication account

Data Retention Period

Your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days after you delete your account, unless a longer retention period is required by law or for security and abuse-prevention purposes.
