Request Data Deletion

Flap does not require account creation or login. However, we may store limited data such as your name, uploaded images, and invite-related data to provide app functionality.

Important: Once your request is processed, your data will be permanently deleted. This process may take up to 30 days.

If you want to delete your data from Flap, please follow the instructions below.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Your name stored on your device
  • Images you uploaded
  • Invite data (pose links and related information)
  • Combined images created with others
  • Notification tokens (FCM)

How to Request Deletion

Since Flap does not have a built-in account system, you can request deletion by contacting us via email.

Email: trinitycreation.dev@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your name used in the app (if known)
  • Any invite link or screenshot (optional, helps identify data)
  • Description of what you want deleted

Data Retention

We will delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request. Some data may be retained temporarily for security or legal purposes.

Important Notes

  • If you shared content with others, copies may still exist on their devices
  • We cannot control content saved outside the app (e.g., WhatsApp shares)
  • Deleting the app does not automatically delete cloud data
