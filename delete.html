Request Account Deletion

This page is for users of CampusThreads who want to request deletion of their account and associated data.

Important: Once your deletion request is processed, your account and data will be permanently deleted. Deletions are completed within 30 days of request for security and verification purposes.

If you can still access the app, you can also delete your account directly from the app’s Settings. Otherwise, use the form below.

How to Delete Your Account from the App

  1. Open the CampusThreads app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap on Delete account
  4. Confirm your choice

Once confirmed, your account will be scheduled for deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, college, avatar/photo)
  • Your posts, comments, and uploaded images
  • Your likes and interactions
  • Your authentication account

Data Retention Period

Your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days after your deletion request, unless a longer retention period is required by law or for security and abuse-prevention purposes.

Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion using the form below. This will open your email app with the details filled in.

By submitting this request, you confirm that you are the owner of the account associated with the provided email address.
